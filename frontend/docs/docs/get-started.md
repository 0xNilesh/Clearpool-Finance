# Get Started

This guide will help you get started with Clearpool Finance and make your first investment.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet)
- Some cryptocurrency (ETH or tokens on Mantle Testnet for testing)
- Basic understanding of DeFi concepts

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. Visit the [Clearpool Finance App](https://app.clearpool.finance)
2. Click on "Sign In" or "Get Started" button
3. Select your wallet from the connection modal
4. Approve the connection request in your wallet
5. Switch to Mantle Testnet (for testing) when prompted

## Making Your First Investment

### Step 1: Browse Available Funds

Once connected, you'll see a list of available funds on the main dashboard. Each fund displays:

- **Name** and **Category**
- **Rating** (out of 5.0)
- **1 Year Return** and **All Time Returns**
- **AUM** (Assets Under Management)
- **Trader Experience** (years)

### Step 2: Filter and Sort Funds

Use the filter button to refine your search by:
- AUM range
- Minimum trader experience
- Category

You can also sort funds by any of the displayed metrics by clicking the sort icons next to column headers.

### Step 3: Select a Fund

Click on any fund row to view detailed information including:
- Performance charts
- Fund composition
- Historical returns
- Fund manager details

### Step 4: Invest

1. Enter the amount you want to invest
2. Review the transaction details
3. Click "Buy" or "Invest"
4. Approve the transaction in your wallet
5. Wait for confirmation

### Step 5: Monitor Your Investment

After investing, you can:
- View your portfolio in the Portfolio section
- Track your investment performance
- Monitor returns and NAV updates
- Request withdrawals anytime
